Why lymphatic drainage

Your lymphatic system carries fluid and small particles through one-way vessels just under the skin. Unlike blood, lymph has no pump — it moves with breath, motion, and gentle external pressure. When the flow slows, the face can hold visible puffiness, less light, and tension along the jaw and around the eyes.

Why prioritize in luteal phase

Progesterone rises in the second half of your cycle. It's associated with softer vessel walls, more water retention, and slower lymph flow. The same routines that worked yesterday can feel less effective now. A short daily lymph routine helps move this — gently, no extreme measures needed.

How the program works

Three short steps, every day for seven days. Each step is sequenced — the body opens lymph pathways before sculpting, then sets the result with cold. Total time: under 18 minutes. Recovery time between steps lets the system respond.

  • Step 1 · Morning lymph 7 min
  • Step 2 · Buccal massage 10 min
  • Step 3 · Cold splash 30 sec

What to expect

Most people see visible depuffing within 2 days and a sharper jawline contour by day 4. Track changes with the AI Face Scan after Step 3 each evening — the metrics ribbon at the top of your home screen reflects what the program is actually moving.
